# 📊 Tableau Dashboard | Sales & Customer Analytics (2023)

## Overview
This repository contains a **two-page interactive Tableau dashboard** designed to analyze business performance across **Sales** and **Customer** dimensions.  
A **common filter panel** (Year, Product, Location) is integrated across both pages for synchronized drill-down and comparative insights.

---

## 📂 Dashboard Pages

### 1️⃣ Sales Dashboard Page
- **Highlights**
  - Dynamic KPIs: Total Sales ($733K, ▲20.36% vs PY), Profit ($93K, ▲14.24% vs PY), Quantity (12,476, ▲26.83% vs PY).  
  - Subcategory analysis: Sales & Profit distribution across product lines.  
  - Trend charts: Monthly sales and profit vs 2022 with highest/lowest month markers.  

![Sales Dashboard](https://github.com/Amit-DataDoc23/Sales-Customer-Dashboard/blob/c3b859543b8dc1a325521e3f1a671743b56e3b9f/Sales%20Dashboard.png)

---

### 2️⃣ Customer Dashboard Page
- **Highlights**
  - Dynamic KPIs: Total Customers (693, ▲8.6% vs PY), Sales per Customer ($1,058.03, ▲10.8% vs PY), Orders (1,687, ▲28.3% vs PY).  
  - Distribution: Customer segmentation by number of orders.  
  - Profitability: Top 10 customers ranked by profit with sales and order history.  

![Customer Dashboard](https://github.com/Amit-DataDoc23/Sales-Customer-Dashboard/blob/c3b859543b8dc1a325521e3f1a671743b56e3b9f/Customer%20dashboard.png)
